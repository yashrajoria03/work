Become an ambassador

Join the seedsnitch ambassador program and become a superhero sidekick that help peers turn ideas into startups and also earn exciting rewards for each referral..

What is it about?

The Seedsnitch Campus Program is a unique opportunity for college students to get involved with the startup ecosystem and earn exciting rewards. As a campus ambassador, you will be helping your peers turn their ideas into successful ventures by connecting them with the resources and support they need to succeed. With Seedsnitch, you'll be able to help build the next big thing while also earning rewards for each successful referral.

How does this work?

We'll call you if we like your application. Campus ambassador duties are simple: Make Seedsnitch known to your peers and locate the students who want to start up but need a little push. We help. We support ambitious college students. Passion drives us, not money. You'll be paid Rs. 5,000 if we fund your proposed startup idea. How do I find a good idea? Simply trust your gut. It's probably too excellent. It's probably the next big thing if you're thinking, "Oh, my goodness, this is it!"
