What is seedsnitch?

Seedsnitch is a platform dedicated to helping college students transform their ideas into successful ventures. Being aware of the unique challenges that a student might come across while starting up, we are here to bridge the gap through guidance and Communitys essential to overcome them. We are a Community with a stern belief that when entrepreneurs come together sharing their share of experiences and support, great things happen.

Why Choose Us?

Seed funding and pre-seed funding opportunities: We provide the initial funding required to get your business off the ground and assist with the early phases of execution.

Mentorship and guidance from experienced entrepreneurs: Our team of experienced entrepreneurs, industry professionals, and educators provides guidance and support to help you navigate the startup world and achieve your goals.

8-week cohort program: Our comprehensive 8-week cohort program covers everything from ideation to execution to accelerated growth and prepares you for the startup world.

Campus ambassador program:The program, which is open to all college students, is an excellent way to gain valuable experience in the startup world, network with like-minded individuals, and earn extra money.
