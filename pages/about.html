We are SeedSnitch, a platform dedicated to helping college students transform their ideas into successful ventures. We are aware of the unique challenges that a student might come across while starting up, and we are here to bridge the gap through guidance and resources essential to overcoming them. Our dedication to fostering the success of young entrepreneurs is deeply rooted. We are passionate about helping young people succeed. We offer a wide range of services to help startups succeed, including seed funding and pre-seed funding opportunities, mentorship, and guidance from experienced entrepreneurs. We are a community with a stern belief that when entrepreneurs come together to share their share of experiences and support, great things happen. So, whether you are just starting out or are well on your way to building the next big thing, join us on this thriving journey and let us help turn your dreams into reality. With SeedSnitch, the possibilities are endless. guidance and resources essential to overcoming them.

Why Choose Us?

Seed funding and pre-seed funding opportunities: We provide the initial funding needed to get your business off the ground and help you with your first steps of execution.

Mentorship and guidance from experienced entrepreneurs: Our team of experienced entrepreneurs, industry professionals, and educators provides guidance and support to help you navigate the startup world and achieve your goals.

8-week cohort program: Our comprehensive 8-week cohort program covers everything from ideation to execution to accelerated growth and prepares you for the startup world.

Campus ambassador program: A program open to all college students, this program is a great way to gain valuable experience in the startup world, network with like-minded individuals, and earn extra money.
